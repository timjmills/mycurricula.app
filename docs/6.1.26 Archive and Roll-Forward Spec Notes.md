# Archive & Roll-Forward — spec notes (capturing owner decisions)

> Core Principle #7 (CLAUDE.md): "Reusable year-over-year. Plans archive and
> roll forward." This file captures the owner's decisions as they're made, ahead
> of a full ultraplan + build. Dated 2026-06-01.

## What the owner asked for

- **Archive** at any granularity: a whole **school year**, or selected
  **subjects / units / lessons / boards** within it. Archiving takes **all data
  associated** with the selection (lessons, completion, notes, resources,
  boards/widgets).
- **Copy / roll forward** at the same granularity: a whole year **or**
  cherry-picked subjects/units/lessons/boards — onto the **current year** OR a
  **new calendar year**. Per subject/unit/specific-lesson selection.

## UX model (owner decision, 2026-06-01)

Roll-forward has TWO dimensions the teacher controls:

- **Scope** — WHICH curriculum subtree: whole year, or cherry-picked
  subjects / units / specific lessons / boards.
- **Data types** — WHICH layers of each item come across, via an **Advanced
  settings** panel. **Default = copy everything** ("general it will do it all");
  the Advanced panel lets you UNCHECK types you don't want.

  Advanced data-type toggles (all ON by default except completion):
  - Lesson content (title, objective, directions) — on
  - Resources (attached links/files) — on
  - Standards tags — on
  - Boards & widgets — on
  - Teacher notes — on (open Q: personal vs team notes)
  - **Completion — always OFF + not user-toggleable** (locked rule #1 below).

So the simple path is one click ("copy the whole year forward"); the Advanced
panel is there when a teacher wants only, say, lessons-without-boards, or
content-without-resources.

## Locked decisions

1. **Completion is NOT copied on roll-forward** (2026-06-01). A rolled-forward
   plan resets: every copied lesson is "not done", `reason_not_done` cleared, no
   `completion_status` rows. Completion needs to be earned again in the new year.
   Completion is NOT an Advanced toggle — it is always excluded.
   The original completion stays with the ARCHIVED year as the historical record.

## Open questions (to resolve before build)

- Does **archive** remove the selection from the active/working view, or just
  tag it `archived_at` and hide it behind a filter? (Likely: tag + hide, with an
  "Archived" view to browse/restore.)
- On roll-forward into a **new calendar year**, do week numbers shift/remap to
  the new year's calendar, or copy verbatim (same week numbers)?
- Roll-forward **conflict handling**: if the target year already has a
  lesson/board in that slot, skip / overwrite / duplicate-alongside?
- Do **boards** roll forward as TEAM boards, PERSONAL boards, or mirror their
  source scope? (Boards carry the forking model — §13.1.)
- What carries with a lesson on copy: notes? personal forks, or just master?
  (Likely: master content only; personal forks are per-teacher and per-year.)

## Schema implications (additive, when built)

- `school_years` already exists (the year entity). Roll-forward is a
  "source year → target year" deep-copy of the selected curriculum subtree with
  fresh ids (deterministic or random) and NO completion rows.
- Add `archived_at timestamptz` to the archivable entities (units, lessons,
  boards) — additive, nullable; an archived row is filtered from active views.
- A copy operation (server action / SQL function) that walks
  subject → unit → lesson → (boards/widgets) for the selection and re-inserts
  under the target year, resetting completion.
