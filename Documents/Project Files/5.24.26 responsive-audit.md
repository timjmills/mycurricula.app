**Date:** 2026-05-24

**Method:** Static read of every primary surface + chrome component (no browser test)
**Status:** **Conditional pass on desktop; fails on phone.**

> CLAUDE.md §4 (the new rule) requires every page/view/feature to work at
> three viewport tiers — Phone 360–480px, Tablet 600–900px, Desktop
> 1024–1920px — with no document-level horizontal scroll, all primary
> controls reachable, ≥44px touch targets on phone/tablet, and sticky
> chrome ≤30% of viewport height on phone. Work is only "done" once
> verified at all three tiers (DevTools device emulation is fine).

## Critical findings (must fix before beta)

### RES-CRIT-001 — Weekly grid forces document-level horizontal scroll on tablet & phone
- **Surface:** `components/grid/WeeklyGrid.module.css` (the `.grid` rule)
- **Root cause:** `grid-template-columns: 132px repeat(5, minmax(190px, 1fr))` plus a hard `min-width: 1082px` on `.grid`. Every viewport narrower than ~1082px forces the document to scroll sideways.
- **Violates:** CLAUDE.md §4 — "No document-level horizontal scroll at any tier."
- **Acceptance:** at ≤900px the Weekly view either (a) collapses to a vertical day-stacked list (one section per day, lessons listed within), (b) shows only the day for the focused day with a horizontal day picker, or (c) keeps the grid but moves it inside a CONTAINED scroll context so the page doesn't scroll. The L1 List view already exists — leveraging it as the auto-fallback at narrow widths is the cheapest fix: at ≤900px, set `viewMode` to `"list"` automatically (or hide Grid choice from the top-bar pill).

### RES-CRIT-002 — Master/Personal toggle disappears below 540px with no fallback
- **Surface:** `components/shell/top-bar.module.css` (`.editToggle { display: none }` at `max-width: 540px`)
- **Root cause:** Wave-2 responsive sweep hid the toggle to fit the bar at narrow widths; no alternative reach was added.
- **Violates:** CLAUDE.md §4 — "all primary controls reachable."
- **Acceptance:** the Master/Personal toggle is reachable at every viewport width — either kept in the top bar (with tighter chrome) OR moved into the avatar menu / a settings shortcut on phone.

## High findings

### RES-HIGH-001 — Year sidebar (140px fixed) never collapses
- **Surface:** `components/year/YearView.module.css` (no media rule on the sidebar)
- **Impact:** at 360px the YearSidebar consumes ~39% of the viewport; the roadmap is cramped.
- **Acceptance:** at ≤720px the YearSidebar either collapses to a horizontal scroll strip (like SubjectView already does at 720px) or hides behind a toggle in the page header.

### RES-HIGH-002 — Top-bar overflow risk at 600–900px
- **Surface:** `components/shell/top-bar.tsx` + css
- **Impact:** The current breakpoints (1280/1024/768/540/480px) work, but the 600–900px band is right at the boundary — depending on which controls remain, the bar can crowd to the right edge.
- **Acceptance:** at 600–900px every visible control has ≥8px of breathing room from the right edge of the viewport.

## Medium findings

### RES-MED-001 — Daily week strip may overflow on 360px
- **Surface:** `components/daily/DailyView.module.css` `.weekStrip`
- **Impact:** Five day pills with `grid-auto-flow: column` and generous padding; on 360px they may exceed available width without a wrap or scroll fallback.
- **Fix:** verify at 360px; if pills exceed width, add `overflow-x: auto` to `.weekStrip` (internal scroll is allowed by the spec) or compress pill padding/font under 480px.

### RES-MED-002 — Subject sidebar at 360px (before the 720px row-collapse rule)
- **Surface:** `components/subject/SubjectView.module.css` (220px fixed sidebar)
- **Impact:** at 360–540px the sidebar takes ~60% of width.
- **Fix:** at ≤540px, hide the sidebar behind a toggle OR compress to ~120px.

### RES-MED-003 — Subscale touch targets across the chrome
| Element | Current size | Should be |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `.iconBtn` (top bar) | 36 × 36 | ≥44 × 44 |
| `.collapseAllBtn` (Daily) | ~28h | ≥44h |
| `.addLessonBtn` (Daily) | 28 × 28 | ≥44 × 44 |
| `.railToggleBtn` (Weekly shell) | 28w × 44h | 44 × 44 |
| `.modeToggleBtn` (Daily rail) | 26 × 22 | ≥44 × 44 |
| `.closeBtn` (right panel) | 28 × 28 | ≥44 × 44 |

**Fix:** inflate hit areas to ≥44 × 44px. Pure visual size can stay small via the padding-with-negative-margin trick (`padding: 11px; margin: -11px`) so layout is unchanged.

### RES-MED-004 — Year stat strip stays 2-up below 480px
- **Surface:** `components/year/YearView.module.css` stat-strip grid (currently `repeat(4, 1fr)` → `repeat(2, 1fr)` at 900px).
- **Fix:** add a 480px breakpoint that drops to `repeat(1, 1fr)` or `repeat(2, 1fr)` with tighter padding.
