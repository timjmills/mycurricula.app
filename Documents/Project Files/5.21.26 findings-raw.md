# MyCurricula UI/UX Audit — Raw Findings (working notes)

Date: 2026-05-21
Tester: Claude (computer-use + Chrome MCP)
Viewport tested: 1440x900 desktop (browser inner 1296x830)
Routes covered: /weekly, /daily, /subject
Build: Next.js (CDN observed), Supabase auth, Cloudflare in front

## Page architecture observed
- Top bar (52px tall, 1280px wide): MyCurricula logo + Grade 5 badge | filter-panel toggle | Weekly/Daily/Subject tabs | Schedule/Unit/Year (labeled "SOON" - not yet built) | Previous/Next week chevrons | "Week 12" label | "This week" button | undo/redo | Personal/Master toggle | Simple/Task/Grid view-mode pill | search input | to-do icon | comments icon (3 unread) | profile avatar | sign-out
- Left icon rail (55px wide): Today (active), Schedule, To-dos, Year/month overview, Voice note, Settings
- Center: Weekly grid (1082px wide rendered)
- Right rail: 320px fixed-position widgets (Resources, To-do List, Day Shoutbox)
- Console: clean on load (no errors)
- Network: 32 requests, all 200/204
- Days rendered: Sun, Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu only (5-day Sun-Thu working week — looks intentional for Middle East schools)

## Findings catalogue (will be sorted into final report)

### LAYOUT-001 — Right rail overlaps the grid at 1440px viewport [HIGH]
- The fixed right rail (320px) sits at left:960 to right:1281, while the lesson grid extends from left:76 to right:1158.
- Result: the rail overlaps the grid by ~198px, fully or partially covering the Wednesday and Thursday columns until the grid is horizontally scrolled.
- Even when the rail is empty/collapsed via the chevron, it still reserves the full 320px on the right.
- Expected: rail should push content (flex layout) or be a true overlay that auto-dismisses; at this viewport teachers cannot see Wed/Thu without scrolling sideways.

### LAYOUT-002 — Sticky subject column ghosts over scrolled-under content [HIGH]
- When the grid is horizontally scrolled, the Math/Reading/Writing row labels remain in place (sticky) but their backgrounds are semi-transparent, so the underlying Sunday-column cards bleed through as ghost text.
- Reproducible at /weekly, scroll horizontally on the grid.
- Fix: give `.WeeklyGrid_cornerCell__tDuO3` and the row-head cells an opaque background that matches the surrounding canvas.

### LAYOUT-003 — Title bar pills bleed off the right edge [LOW/MEDIUM]
- "SimpleTaskGrid" pill at top right is cut off; the "G" in "Grid" sits flush with the window edge, no padding margin.
- The Personal•Master dots can be ambiguous: looks like a bullet glyph between two buttons rather than active-state indicator.

### LAYOUT-004 — Resource thumbnails inside the rail have unreadable titles [MEDIUM]
- Cards in Resources panel labeled "Fracti…", "What is a F…", "Fractions O…", "Khan Acad…" are ellipsised at ~9 chars.
- The two-up grid is too narrow to read titles; rail width 320px / 2 = 152px per card.
- No tooltip on hover (tested) — title is gone unless you switch to list view.
- Tag chips (YOUTUBE, LINK, DOC, etc.) are uppercase and visually heavier than the title text.

### NAV-001 — Previous/next week chevron is duplicated [LOW]
- One pair in the top bar ("Week 12" with chevrons + "This week") and another pair inside the weekly grid header ("Today" with chevrons).
- Both work identically. Two sets of identical controls within 80px vertical distance creates choice paralysis and wastes vertical space.

### NAV-002 — "This week" button disabled when on current week [GOOD]
- Correctly disabled at Week 12 (current).
- Becomes active when navigating to Week 11. (Verified.)

### NAV-003 — Top-bar tabs "Schedule SOON / Unit SOON / Year SOON" are not interactive [INFO]
- "SOON" badge inside tab label is in a smaller, lighter font. They look clickable (same shape as Weekly/Daily/Subject).
- Hover state did not appear (not tested formally — to confirm).
- Suggest disabling them visually (lower opacity) so they read clearly as not-yet-available.

### CONTENT-001 — Card titles truncate at 2 lines with ellipsis [INFO]
- "Multiplying a whol…" cuts at word boundary, no tooltip on hover.
- "Fractions as division — bake…" cuts mid-word.

### CONTENT-002 — Card sections in expanded mode are great [POSITIVE]
- "I CAN" / "DIRECTIONS" / "NOTES" / "RESOURCES" / "STANDARDS" labels in colored chips, content readable.
- Resources show with mini-icons + file-type chip (Video / Link / Doc / Slides / PDF) — clear visual language.

### STATE-001 — Resource count and chat are date/week scoped [POSITIVE]
- Switching from Week 12 → Week 11 shows "Resources · Week 11" (3 resources) and Day Shoutbox empties to "Nothing from the team yet today." Good scoping.

### COPY-001 — Empty cell hint mixes drag and click [GOOD/POSITIVE]
- "Drag a lesson here or click +" — clear, teaches both interactions in one line.

### A11Y-001 — ARIA labeling is strong overall [POSITIVE]
- Buttons and links have descriptive aria-labels: "Mark \"Equivalent fractions warm-up\" — current status not_done", "Drag to move this lesson", "Previous week", "Open comments panel (3 unread)" etc.
- Grid has role=grid, columnheaders have role=columnheader.
- Day headers say "SundaySun" without a separator — screen reader will read "Sundaysun"; add a space or use aria-label "Sunday, Sun".

### A11Y-002 — Hidden affordance: lesson card body text uses faded colour [TO VERIFY]
- The body paragraph copy in expanded cards (#404 or similar grey on tinted background) needs contrast measurement against WCAG AA.
