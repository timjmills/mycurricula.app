Curriculum planning, made warm

Structured like a planner,

A design language that fuses the dense, color-coded clarity of a curriculum dashboard with the soft gradients and rounded forms of a modern creative site. Scales to a dozen-plus subjects, with color that flows down every unit, week and lesson.

Subject scale

The planner is navigated by color, so the palette scales to the 12 to 15 subjects a school might run. Colors are muted and harmonious in the style of the White Rose grade bands. Each slot carries a solid (headers, icons, accents), a tint (backgrounds, lanes) and an ink (text on tint). Assign any subject to any slot; the four named aliases are just defaults.

Buttons

Pill-shaped throughout. The brand button is indigo blue, honey is the warm hero CTA, secondary and ghost carry lower-priority work.

Components

Inputs

Inputs sit on the warm surface and brighten to white on focus, with a soft brand-tint focus ring instead of a hard outline. Search is pill-shaped to match the dashboard chrome.

Cards & cascade

Each panel sets one trio of variables: --c (a brighter accent for outlines, icon tiles and dots), --ct (the tint fill) and --ck (kept for any colored text). Every chip carries a colored outline and a soft shadow, with dark text for legibility. The active unit and week take a heavier outline.
