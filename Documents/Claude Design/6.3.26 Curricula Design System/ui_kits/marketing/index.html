Why it feels different

A plan you can actually see.

Every subject owns a color, and that color flows from the subject down to its units, weeks, and lessons. Status is a separate layer — so progress always reads the same, on every subject.

Color is the navigation

Fifteen muted subject hues coexist without clashing. Glance at the board and you know exactly where every subject stands.

Plan the whole year

Lay out subjects into units, weeks, and daily lessons. Drag to reschedule; open any lesson to see objectives, standards, and resources.

Catch-up that's honest

When a lesson slips, it doesn't disappear. Catch-up gathers what moved so you can reschedule, skip, or merge — without losing the thread.

Teach mode, on the board

Project a calm teaching board — now & next, a timer, a noise meter, today's objective — built from the plan you already made.

How it works

From a blank year to a living plan.

1

Add your subjects

Pick subjects and grade. Each one gets a color you can recolor anytime.

2

Break it into units

Drop in units and weeks. The subject color cascades down automatically.

3

Fill the lessons

Add daily lessons with objectives, standards, and the materials you'll use.

4

Teach & adjust

Mark progress, catch up what slipped, and project teach mode in class.
