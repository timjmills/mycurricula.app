mycurricula.app Design System

The brand & product design language — for teachers, by teachers. Dashboard clarity with landing-page warmth, where color is the navigation: every subject owns a hue that cascades to its units, weeks and lessons, and status stays a separate layer. Tokens live in colors_and_type.css.

Typography

Bricolage Grotesque · Plus Jakarta Sans

Headings in Bricolage Grotesque (warm, editorial, tight tracking). UI, body & data in Plus Jakarta Sans (clean, professional). The one uppercase style is the small label/eyebrow.

Iconography

Lucide-style line icons

Clean line icons — ~2px stroke, round caps & joins, 24×24, unfilled. Delivered as inline SVG (no icon font). Glyphs inside colored chip tiles are white on solid, accent on soft. Emoji is not used; Unicode appears only as punctuation — the middle dot · and the arrow →.
